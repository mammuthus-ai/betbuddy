Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 25, 2026

PutUpOrShutUp ("we," "our," or "us") operates the PutUpOrShutUp mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account using Google authentication
  • Create or participate in bets
  • Upload profile pictures or proof images
  • Communicate with other users through the App

Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account.

User Content: We collect content you create, upload, or share through the App, including bet descriptions, comments, proof images, and profile information.

Device Information: We automatically collect certain device information including your device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and push notification tokens.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, including features used, bets created or joined, and timestamps of activity.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Create and manage your account
  • Facilitate bet creation, tracking, and resolution
  • Send push notifications about bet updates and activity
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your profile information, bets, and activity are visible to other users of the App as part of normal functionality.
  • Service Providers: We share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf (see Section 5).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Safety: We may disclose information to protect the rights, property, or safety of PutUpOrShutUp, our users, or others.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Google Cloud Platform (Firebase). We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Service Providers

We use the following third-party services that may collect or process your data:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, database, storage, cloud functions, and analytics
  • Google Cloud Messaging: Push notification delivery
  • Google Cloud Vision: Image content moderation for uploaded avatars and proof images
  • Expo / React Native: App framework and over-the-air updates

Each service provider has its own privacy policy governing their use of your data.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You may:

  • Access and Update: View and update your profile information through the App.
  • Delete Account: Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.
  • Push Notifications: Opt out of push notifications through your device settings.
  • Data Export: Request a copy of your personal data by contacting us.

7. Content Moderation

We use automated image analysis (Google Cloud Vision SafeSearch) to scan user-uploaded images (avatars and proof submissions) for inappropriate content including adult, violent, or explicit material. This analysis is performed at the time of upload. Images flagged as inappropriate are rejected and not stored. Google may process these images on their servers in accordance with Google Cloud's data processing terms.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App and updating the "Effective Date" above. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
